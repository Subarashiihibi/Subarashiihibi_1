General Introduction


The first version of ArcGIS is released in 1999 as ACR/INFO. It is developed by ESRI, written in C++. Actually, ArcGIS is a franchise of products including client software, server software, as well as online GIS services which are maintained by ESRI. To fully experience the use of the product, users might have to pay for the services provided by ESRI.
